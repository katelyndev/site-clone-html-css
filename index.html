Innoviatour

Innoviatour, a part of the Supertravel Group,
has been operating in the travel and tourism
market for over 30 years in Latin America.

Now, we are expanding to North America,
bringing our expertise in religious, innovation,
business, and networking itineraries, as well
as tourism representation and much more.

While our specialty lies in the Middle East,
Africa, and Asia, we also operate in
Latin America and Europe, providing warm
and attentive care to every individual,
whether it's a solo traveler, company,
university or school, as well as families,
groups of friends, and religious institutions.

Discover more about our work, as it would
be our pleasure to fulfill your dreams.
